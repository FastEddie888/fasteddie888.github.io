OMNI Credit Card Branch Project

Modernization in creating a new OMNI UI Credit Card Shopping Experience

Project summary

The Credit Card OMNI UI is an internal web application that offers an unified user experience during the Credit Card Application process. It further reduces the time to complete a credit card application to an average of under 5 minutes. With this, we have accomplished our goal of offering unified user experience for customers submitting Credit Card Applications across multiple channels - Branch Bankers, Advisors, and Phone Banking (Wealth Advisors & Private Bankers to come…). The curated unified experience created a new simplified approach built on the updated Wells Fargo design language and modernized components. The entirety of the process designed were handled within Figma and showcased as presentational views to outline how the end to end state performed.

The project captures onboarding of the 4 major credit card products as part of the branch focused digital brochure. Each line of business supported within the channels each have their unique application based data capture and consent processes ending with a submitted application for downstream decision making processes. Each of the channels blended with the overall OMNI UI experience and incorporated federated compliance policies and their own standard operating processes.

The design process involved leveraging each channel's own implementation team to iterate and model the most efficient way possible to meet the 5 minute mark. All the designs would utilize the latest brand standards library of components, design philosophy, and color schematics to stay aligned with the overall enterprise branding strategy utilizing the brand's Fusion design language. A react.js component library was created to allow the development team to utilize the components in a more efficient manner.
