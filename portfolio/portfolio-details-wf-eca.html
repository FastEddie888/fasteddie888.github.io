Consumer Microsite Project

New Credit Card Confirmation microsite module in Shopping onboarding experience

Project summary

Supported the design, business and development teams to curate a templated web based microsite that is consumer facing enabling new card activation for all new cards received by the applicant. The templated microsite supports the new and existing inhouse card catalog and also partnering vendor cards. The consumer microsite is closely aligned with the existing customer's digital experience on the mobile platform as well as the profile authenticated digital experience. As part of the overall support and meeting compliance to external facing web applications mandates, aligned with ADA testing team to ensure governing standards were met and approved prior to production release.

Skills: Customer Experience · Figma (Software) · SASS · User Interface Design · Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) · React.js · User Experience Design · Responsive design · Wireframing · JavaScript
