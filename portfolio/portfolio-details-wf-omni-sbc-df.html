OMNI Dodd Frank Small Business integration Project

Integration of Dodd Frank Compliance into Small Biz Card Application consent workflow

Project summary

Played a key role as design lead collaborating with business and product teams to ensure new Dodd Frank federal regulartory compliance capturing demographic information is integrated seemlessly as part of the consent designs. This new unique process features a primary flow to capture demographic details utilizing a QR Code to initiate WF's mobile app whilst also support a secondary backup flow integrated into the existing experience providing the same consistent data collection format reflected on the mobile counterpart.

All the designs utilizes the latest design system's library of components, design philosophy, and color schematics to stay aligned with the overall enterprise branding strategy utilizing the brand's global design language. A react.js templated mockup was created to allow the engineering team to utilize the components in a more efficient manner to deliver the product more efficiently.

Skills: Customer/User Experience · Figma · SASS/CSS · Responsive User Interface Design · Wireframing · Hifi Mockups · React.js
